# Cloud Organizational Agility

Organizational Agility is the ability to adapt and evolve successfully for internal and external factors.
Is a measure that indicates the responsiveness to change of the organization.
The change is more oriented on market fluctuation rather then [[Cloud scalability|infrastructure]].
