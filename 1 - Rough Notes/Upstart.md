# Upstart

Upstart is a [[Service Manager]] and like [[systemd]] can run, stop and observe the status of [[Daemon]] and launched scripts.

The principal Upstart commands are:
- [[initctl]]
- [[status]]
- [[start]]
- [[stop]]
